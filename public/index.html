NPC tuning guide

Bot configuration

Difficulty changes how effectively a bot uses its personality. Personality changes what the bot tries to prioritize during a round.

Difficulty

Easy
Slower reactions, shorter planning and more mistakes.
Medium
Balanced reactions, planning distance and risk-taking.
Hard
Faster reactions, longer planning and fewer missed chances.

Personality

Survivor
Prefers open space and safer turns over aggressive pressure.
Hunter
Looks for opponents and tries to cut off their movement.
Collector
Values power-ups and may take extra risks to reach them.

Example: a Hard Hunter should feel much more dangerous than an Easy Hunter.
